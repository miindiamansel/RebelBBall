BASKETBALL FOR ALL

At the LA Rebels basketball program, our mission is to ignite the potential within young athletes and propel them towards a future of success. Through comprehensive skill enhancement, collaborative teamwork, and unwavering sportsmanship.

We empower our players to thrive both on and off the court.

With passionate coaching staff committed to cultivating a positive and supportive environment, we pave the way for our players to soar to new heights and transform their dreams of basketball greatness into reality. Join us on this extraordinary journey and discovery the winning edge that sets the LA Rebels apart.

OUR COACH

OSCAR "COACH O" BELLFIELD

Born and raised in Los Angeles, Coach O brings a high level of expertise and knowledge to the game of basketball, focusing on player development and recruitment.

Coach O has 10 years of experience training athletes and coaching on various levels. During the 2022-2023 season, Coach O was assistant coach for the men’s team at LA Valley College. He also coached Westchester High School’s varsity team to win the CIF City Championship in 2020.

As a player, Oscar played for the University of Nevada Las Vegas (UNLV) where he led the men’s team to three NCAA Tournament appearances in four years. He’s also played professionally for the Memphis Grizzlies, New York Knicks, Erie BayHawks and internationally for Cherkasy Mavp where he won the Ukrainian Basketball Championship.
